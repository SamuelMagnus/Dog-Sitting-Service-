Install on Phone

Install PawScheduler on your home screen — works offline, launches full screen like a real app, your data stays on your phone.

iPhone / iPad: Tap the Share button in Safari ↗ then tap "Add to Home Screen"
Android: Tap the menu (⋮) in Chrome then "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"

⚠️ Not working? Opening from Downloads?
Don't open the file directly from your Downloads folder — this blocks the app's storage. Instead:
1. Open Chrome or Samsung Internet
2. Tap the address bar and type the file path, or drag the file into the browser
3. Then use "Add to Home Screen" from the browser menu
